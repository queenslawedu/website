Why choose us

Why Choose Queens College of Law & Management?

Choosing the right institution for your legal education is a crucial decision that will shape your future. At Queens College of Law & Management, we offer a unique blend of legal and managerial education.

Prime Location in Bhanimandal, Lalitpur:Our campus is strategically located in the educational hub of Lalitpur, making it convenient for students to access a wide range of academic, professional, and social opportunities. The vibrant community surrounding the college offers a stimulating environment for learning and growth.

A Comprehensive 5-Year B.A.LL.B Program: Our integrated B.A.LL.B program combines rigorous legal studies with the exploration of social sciences, enabling students to develop a holistic understanding of law in relation to society. This interdisciplinary approach ensures that graduates are well-equipped to handle the complexities of modern legal practice.

State-of-the-Art Facilities: We provide modern facilities, including spacious classrooms, a well-stocked library, moot courtrooms, and computer labs. Our campus is designed to support an interactive and engaging learning experience.

Experienced Faculty: Our distinguished faculty includes legal scholars, practicing lawyers, and experts in various fields of law and management. They bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to the classroom, ensuring that students receive a well-rounded education.

Practical Learning Opportunities: At Queens College, we emphasize experiential learning. Students have the opportunity to participate in moot court competitions, legal aid clinics, internships, and case studies, which prepare them for the practical challenges of the legal profession.

Focus on Ethics and Professionalism: We believe that a strong foundation in ethics and professionalism is crucial for success in the legal field. Our curriculum incorporates these values at every stage, helping students become responsible and ethical legal practitioners.

At Queens College of Law & Management, we are dedicated to shaping the future of legal professionals in Nepal.
