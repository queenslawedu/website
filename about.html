About us

About Queens College of Law & Management

Queens College of Law & Management is a distinguished legal education institution founded with the mission to produce competent, ethical, and socially responsible legal professionals. As a college affiliated with Rajarshi Janak University, we are proud to offer a globally recognized curriculum that balances theoretical knowledge with practical legal training. Our academic programs are designed to address the growing need for skilled professionals in law and management, and we are committed to providing our students with the tools they need to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Our 5-year B.A.LL.B program is tailored to provide students with an interdisciplinary education that covers the core principles of law as well as related disciplines such as political science, sociology, and economics. This comprehensive approach helps students develop critical thinking, analytical reasoning, and leadership skills that are essential for success in the legal profession.

At Queens College of Law & Management, we are dedicated to shaping the future of legal professionals in Nepal.
